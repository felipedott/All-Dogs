Welcome to All Dogs In The City

Dogs need more than just a daycare. That's why we're not just a boarding facility -- we're a recreational resort for all dogs in the city.

When it comes to NYC, "big city" and "fast paced" fall short to describe the busy lives we lead. All Dogs In The City is dedicated to helping dog lovers treat their pets to top-notch doggo daycare and boarding when schedules get tight. Whether your best friend is with us for a day or a week, we truly love pampering your pups and treating them to vacations of their own. And with the space to play (and even swim!) with other furry friends right here in our facility, your pet may even be sad to leave. So, whether you find yourself needing to stay late at the office or skip town for peace and quiet, you can count on us to care for you pup as our own -- and that goes for all dogs in the city.

Our Services

For superior grooming, daycare, boarding, dog walking and pick-up services for your fabulous dog, All Dogs In The City is the place to be. Treat your pet to a full pampering experience at our parlor, where expert stylists provide meticulous grooming services that fit your budget and lifestyle. Our passion is to help owners improve the pets' quality of life. We are a specialty facility, determined on providing our furry friends with personalized care and attention, they truly deserve. Our spacious facility has a temperature-controlled pool along with a sanitation system that uses ultraviolet light to keep the water chemical free. We also have a private and safe outdoor space for our dogs, where they can play and rest in a natural atmosphere.
